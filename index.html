Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each srystal is hidden from you until click on it.
